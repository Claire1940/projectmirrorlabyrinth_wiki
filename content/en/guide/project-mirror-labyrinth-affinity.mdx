<Section icon={Swords} title="Elemental Affinity Combat Mechanics">

Elemental matchups dictate the flow of combat within the labyrinth. Every character and enemy is assigned one of six elemental affinities: Pyro, Hydro, Aero, Geo, Light, or Dark. Understanding these relationships allows players to bypass high defensive stats on tougher bosses and clear stages much faster.

When a character holds an elemental advantage over an enemy, they deal significantly increased damage while mitigating incoming attacks. Conversely, attacking an enemy with a disadvantageous element results in reduced damage output and increased vulnerability.

| Attacking Element | Defending Element | Damage Multiplier | Matchup Status |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Pyro | Geo | 1.50x | Advantage |
| Hydro | Pyro | 1.50x | Advantage |
| Aero | Hydro | 1.50x | Advantage |
| Geo | Aero | 1.50x | Advantage |
| Light | Dark | 1.50x | Mutual Advantage |
| Dark | Light | 1.50x | Mutual Advantage |
| Pyro | Hydro | 0.75x | Disadvantage |

Light and Dark elements operate on a mutual advantage system. They deal increased damage to each other but remain neutral against the four basic elements. This setup makes Light and Dark characters highly versatile generalists for mixed-element stages.

<Callout type="warning" title="Disadvantage Penalties">
Attacking an enemy while at an elemental disadvantage reduces your critical strike chance by twenty-five percent. Avoid using single-element teams in stages with diverse enemy compositions to prevent severe damage drop-offs.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Affinity Level Progression and Milestones">

Advancing through the affinity ranks requires increasing amounts of experience points. Every milestone reached provides a permanent boost to the character's core attributes. These bonuses are added directly to the character's base stats before gear multipliers are applied, making them highly valuable.

The progression system features ten distinct levels, with major breakthrough rewards positioned at levels three, six, and ten.

<Section icon={Map} title="Step-by-Step Affinity Farming Route">

To maximize your daily affinity gains without wasting stamina, follow this structured routine. This route prioritizes high-yield daily activities and cost-effective shop purchases.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Collect Base Dormitory Points">
    Visit the dormitory area immediately after logging in. Tap on the floating bubbles above your characters to claim accumulated passive affinity points and trigger random daily chat events.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Purchase Daily Shop Gifts">
    Navigate to the general store and purchase the three discounted gift boxes. These boxes cost standard gold coins and should always be purchased before the daily shop reset.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Clear Daily Resource Stages">
    Spend your daily stamina on the highest level resource stages. Ensure the characters you want to level up are placed in the active combat party, as passive reserve slots receive fewer points.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Distribute Targeted Gifts">
    Open your character screen and distribute your collected gifts. Prioritize leveling one character to milestone three or six rather than spreading gifts evenly across your entire roster.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Stamina Sweeps">
Using sweep tickets on completed stages still awards full affinity points to all characters currently slotted in the selected team. Use this feature to quickly farm affinity for low-level characters without risking defeat in high-level stages.
</Callout>
