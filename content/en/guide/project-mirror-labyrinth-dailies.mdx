<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Check Official Community Status">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Redeem New Codes">
    Enter the game and open the **Codes** menu from the left side of the screen. Enter any new codes exactly as written, as they are case-sensitive.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Initiate Luxcavation">
    Use the **TAB menu** or the **NO ENTRY gate** to start a Luxcavation run. These provide the most consistent daily Fragment income for the shortest time investment.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Manage Reflections">
    Check the current **Targeted Reflection** banners. If a banner features an Outfit that complements your current deck, spend your accumulated Fragments here.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Defragment and Upgrade">
    Visit the Outfit menu to check if you have enough **Condense** to Defragment your main gear. This unlocks passive abilities that are crucial for late-game survival.
  </Step>
</Steps>
