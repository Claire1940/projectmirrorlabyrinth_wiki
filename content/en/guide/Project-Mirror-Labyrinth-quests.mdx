<Section icon={Swords} title="Step-by-Step Quest Completion Guide">

To efficiently clear all three major quest lines, follow this structured path to optimize your combat runs and minimize turn counts.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Clear the Deici Library Trials">
    Locate the Deici Librarian inside the Deici Library. Trim your deck down to exactly 5 cards. Head into the Backstreets or Library encounters to defeat 10 enemies and clear 5 total encounters.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Advance Hana Building Research">
    Visit the LCE Researcher inside the Hana Building. Hunt down Abnormalities across different difficulty ratings: defeat 1 Zayin, 5 Teth/He, and 3 Waw-level threats to unlock higher research tiers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Optimize Devyat Delivery Operations">
    Speak with the Devyat Rep inside the Devyat Building. Complete any stage twice, activate the Devyat passive to repeat the feat, and optimize your strategy to finish a stage in under 20 turns.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Reward Optimization">
Completing the Devyat Rep's quest line unlocks the **Speedy Courier** passive. This passive is highly recommended for players looking to speed up their stage runs and maximize delivery efficiency.
</Callout>
