<Section icon={Map} title="How to Unlock Project Mirror Labyrinth Titles">

Unlocking titles requires a combination of progression, exploration, and combat mastery. The game features a structured tracking system that monitors your achievements across all characters on your account. Once a title is unlocked, it becomes available for all characters on that profile, allowing new characters to benefit from high-level stat boosts early in their progression.

To unlock and equip your first set of titles, you must navigate through the achievements menu and meet the specific criteria set by the labyrinth guides.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access Labyrinth Records">
    Open the main menu and navigate to the Achievements tab, then select the Labyrinth Records sub-category to view all available titles.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Fulfill Unlock Criteria">
    Complete the designated task, which may involve defeating a specific boss, clearing a floor within a time limit, or collecting rare shards.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Claim the Reward">
    Once the criteria are met, return to the Labyrinth Records menu and manually claim the title to add it to your character profile.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Equip the Title">
    Navigate to your character sheet, click on the Title slot under your name, and select the unlocked title to activate its stat bonuses.
  </Step>
</Steps>
