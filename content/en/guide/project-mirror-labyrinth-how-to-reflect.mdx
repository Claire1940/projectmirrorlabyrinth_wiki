### What is a Reflection?

Reflections are essentially the gacha mechanics of Project Mirror Labyrinth. Instead of summoning characters, you reflect to manifest **Outfits**. Outfits are crucial because they dictate your character's starting stats, passive perks, and overall build synergy. Whether you are running a high-damage offensive deck or a stable defensive build, your choice of Outfit will heavily influence your playstyle.

To perform a Reflection, you must spend **Fragments** or specialized tickets. Fragments can be earned by completing dungeon runs or by redeeming promotional codes provided by the developers.

<Callout type="info" title="System Basics">
Outfits drawn from Reflections are not just cosmetic. They are the backbone of your character's build, providing the passive abilities necessary to tackle higher-difficulty Luxcavations and deep dungeon runs.
</Callout>

Your focus in Project Mirror Labyrinth will shift dramatically as you progress from a beginner navigating your first dungeon to an advanced player optimizing high-difficulty runs.

### Key Strategy Tips

- **Prioritize Defense Early**: When starting out, do not neglect defense cards. While attack cards are necessary to clear encounters, defense cards keep you alive through mistimed turns or bad card draws.
- **Save High-Value Tickets**: Tickets like the **SR Guaranteed Mega Ticket** and **Mega Reflection Ticket** are rare. Do not spend them on standard banners unless you are looking to build a baseline collection of Outfits.
- **Restart After Updates**: The developers update the game frequently (with scheduled maintenance around **2:00 P.M. every Tuesday**). If you notice that menus are unresponsive or a newly redeemed code is not working, exit the game and rejoin a fresh, updated server.
