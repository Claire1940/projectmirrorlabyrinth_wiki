<Section icon={Swords} title="Understanding Core Combat & Gameplay Mechanics">

Combat in Project Mirror Labyrinth relies on balancing offensive pressure with defensive stability. Players draw from a customizable deck of attack and defense cards. Successfully clearing rooms requires synergy rather than playing random cards.

### Card Types and Deck Synergies

Developing a balanced deck is the difference between completing a dungeon and failing on the first floor.

| Card Category | Primary Function | Strategic Use Case |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Attack Cards** | Deal direct damage to enemies | Bursting down high-threat targets quickly |
| **Defense Cards** | Generate block and mitigate damage | Surviving heavy enemy attacks and conserving HP |
| **Combo Cards** | Provide buffs, draw, or utility | Enhancing the efficiency of your main cards |

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Dungeon Exploration & Troubleshooting">

Dungeons are the heart of the game. If you encounter issues starting a run or redeeming rewards, keep these community-tested troubleshooting steps in mind.

### Dungeon Entry Methods

There are two primary ways to initiate a dungeon:
1. **The UI Route**: Press **TAB** to toggle the menu, then click **Play**.
2. **The World Route**: Walk to the physical **NO ENTRY** gate in the lobby and interact with it.

### Troubleshooting Common Issues

| Issue | Potential Cause | Recommended Solution |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Codes Not Working** | Case-sensitivity or Group requirements | Verify capitalization and ensure you have joined the Roblox group. |
| **Dungeon Won't Load** | Server desync or active maintenance | Rejoin a fresh server or check the Tuesday maintenance schedule. |
| **UI Menus Frozen** | Roblox client lag | Press **TAB** twice to reset the interface, or restart the client. |

<Callout type="warning" title="Maintenance Notice">
The game undergoes scheduled weekly maintenance every Tuesday at approximately 2:00 P.M. EST. Dungeon matchmaking and code redemptions may be temporarily disabled during this window.
</Callout>
