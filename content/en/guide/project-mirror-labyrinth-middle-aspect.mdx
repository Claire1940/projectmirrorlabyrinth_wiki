<Section icon={Map} title="Understanding the project mirror labyrinth middle aspect">

The mid-game phase of the labyrinth introduces players to complex spatial puzzles and aggressive scaling mechanics. Reaching this zone requires a solid grasp of how mirror reflections interact with player positioning and skill casting. Unlike the outer zones where raw damage can carry you through encounters, the middle aspect demands strategic build pathing and careful management of environmental hazards.

Environmental hazards in this zone include temporal decay and mirror reflections. Temporal decay slowly drains your resource pool if you stand still for too long, encouraging constant mobility. Mirror reflections, on the other hand, copy your attacks and project them back at you if you fail to align your character with the active prism nodes scattered across the chamber floors.

| Zone | Level Range | Key Hazard | Primary Material |
| Outer Aspect | 1-30 | Basic Traps | Shard Dust |
| Middle Aspect | 31-70 | Mirror Reflection, Temporal Decay | Refined Quartz, Aspect Core |
| Inner Aspect | 71-100 | Void Collapse | Void Crystal |

To bypass these hazards, you must locate the prism nodes in each chamber and activate them in the correct sequence. Activating a node temporarily disables the reflection hazard, giving you a short window to unleash your high-damage skills. It is highly recommended to prioritize mobility-focused utility skills during this phase to avoid both temporal decay and reflected projectiles.
