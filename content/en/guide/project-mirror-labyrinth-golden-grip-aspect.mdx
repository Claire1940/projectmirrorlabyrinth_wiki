<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Project Mirror Labyrinth Golden Grip Aspect Mechanics">

The **project mirror labyrinth golden grip aspect** stands out as one of the most versatile utility-offensive modifiers in the game. It alters the fundamental handling properties of heavy, slow-swinging armaments. By equipping this aspect, your character gains access to enhanced weight distribution, effectively reducing the recovery frames after heavy attacks and increasing overall combat mobility.

At its core, the aspect converts a portion of your total weight capacity into raw swing velocity. When wielding two-handed weapons, the wind-up animations are shortened, allowing you to execute rapid follow-up strikes that would otherwise be impossible. Additionally, the aspect introduces a unique gold-generation mechanic, granting a chance to drop gold piles whenever you execute a critical hit or break an enemy's posture.
