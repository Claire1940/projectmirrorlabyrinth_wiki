<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step: How to Run Your First Dungeon">

Entering a dungeon and surviving your first few battles can be daunting. Follow this step-by-step pipeline to start your run and build a stable deck.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Dungeon Gate">
    In the main lobby, you can start a run by pressing **TAB** on your keyboard and clicking **Play**. Alternatively, walk up to and interact with the physical **NO ENTRY** gate located in the game world.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Draft Your Starting Deck">
    Upon entering, you will receive a basic set of attack and defense cards. Do not neglect defensive options; blocking incoming damage is crucial for surviving deep runs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Engage in Tactical Combat">
    Combat is turn-based. Analyze enemy intents displayed above their heads. Play defense cards to mitigate heavy attacks and use your attack cards during openings to whittle down their health.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Collect and Spend Fragments">
    Clear encounters to gather Fragments. Once back in the lobby, spend these Fragments on the Reflection banners to roll for stronger Outfits.
  </Step>
</Steps>
