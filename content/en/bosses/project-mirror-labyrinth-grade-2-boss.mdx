### Core Fight Dynamics

To survive this boss, you must manage your combat resources. The table below outlines the core mechanics you will encounter during the fight.

| Combat Mechanic | How It Works | Strategic Response |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Action Slots** | Boss starts with multiple attack slots, allowing 4 to 5 attacks per turn. | Use high-value clash cards to match each incoming slot. |
| **Helper Nuggets** | Weak but annoying minions that attempt to distract your primary damage dealers. | Focus down helper units rapidly to isolate the boss. |
| **Sanity Posture** | Sanity acts as your posture. It halves after every fight, leaving you vulnerable. | Prioritize passive perks like Breathing II to guarantee turn-one posture. |
| **Stagger Thresholds**| Inflicting enough damage breaks the enemy's stance, skipping their next turn. | Chain stagger thresholds to lock the boss out of high-rolling attacks. |

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Boss Fight Walkthrough">

Defeating the **project mirror labyrinth grade 2 boss** requires a disciplined approach. Follow these precise phases to dismantle the boss's strategy.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Clear the Support Nuggets First">
    Begin the battle by focusing all your multi-dice attacks on the helper nuggets. Eliminating them quickly reduces the total number of incoming attacks per turn, making clash management much easier.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Prioritize Clashing Above All">
    Never leave an enemy attack unopposed. Use your highest-rolling attack cards to clash with the boss's action slots. Winning a clash completely negates the boss's damage for that slot.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Chain Stagger Thresholds">
    Once the helper units are gone, coordinate your attacks to push the boss past its stagger threshold. A staggered boss loses all its actions for the next turn, providing a massive damage window.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Manage Posture and Sanity">
    If your sanity drops too low, use defensive Block cards to stabilize. Do not play risky, low-rolling attack cards when your posture is compromised.
  </Step>
</Steps>
