<Section icon={Map} title="Combat Rotations & Tactical Strategies">

Navigating combat encounters as a solo player requires patience and precise execution. Since you are the sole target for all enemies in the room, understanding how to group mobs together and manage their attack animations is key to clearing rooms without taking unnecessary damage.

Use the environment to your advantage by breaking line of sight with ranged enemies. This forces them to run toward your position, grouping them up for efficient area-of-effect attacks. When facing boss monsters, focus on learning their attack patterns and visual cues to maximize the timing of your counter-attacks.
