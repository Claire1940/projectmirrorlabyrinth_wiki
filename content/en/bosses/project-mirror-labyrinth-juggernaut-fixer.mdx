<Section icon={Swords} title="Core Skills and Combat Mechanics">

Understanding the skill set of the **project mirror labyrinth juggernaut fixer** is vital for maximizing DPS. The character relies on high-velocity attacks and a unique "Intent Power" system (often referred to as Coin Power) that scales based on status potency.

| Skill Name | Base Power | Primary Effect | Ammo Cost |
|------------|------------|----------------|-----------|
| Knife Combat | 5 | Basic Pierce damage, minor Rupture | 0 |
| Tommy Burst | 7 | Multi-hit burst, applies Wounded | 4 |
| Gut Jab | 8 | High impact, inflicts 4 Wounded | 0 |
| Unload | 12 | Spends all remaining ammo for massive Rupture | All |
| Evade | N/A | Heals surplus ammo and removes overheat | 0 |

The standout skill is undoubtedly **Unload**. By spending all 32 rounds of ammo, the Juggernaut Fixer inflicts Rupture potency equal to half the ammo spent to the target on the following turn. This allows for a massive burst of damage that can bypass high-defense targets.

<Callout type="tip" title="Ammo Conservation">
Do not use Unload immediately. Build up Rupture and Wounded stacks using Gut Jab and Tommy Burst first to ensure the final burst from Unload benefits from existing multipliers.
</Callout>

### The Double Punishment Passive
The core of the Juggernaut's utility lies in its passive ability, **Double Punishment**. This passive rewards players for monitoring the target's status icons closely:
- **On Hit (Target has Rupture but no Wounded)**: Inflicts 1 Wounded.
- **On Hit (Target has Wounded but no Rupture)**: Inflicts 3 Rupture.

This creates a self-sustaining loop where the Juggernaut Fixer can keep both debuffs active without needing external support, though teammates who apply the same statuses will accelerate the process significantly.
