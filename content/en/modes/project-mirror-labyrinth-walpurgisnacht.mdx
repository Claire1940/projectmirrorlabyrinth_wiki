<Section icon={Swords} title="New Identity Analysis: Southern Thumb Mafia">

The standout addition in the **project mirror labyrinth walpurgisnacht** update is undoubtedly the Southern Thumb Mafia ID. This unit introduces a sophisticated Ammo system paired with a dual-status effect focus. Unlike standard attackers, the Southern Thumb Mafia relies on a finite resource—32 rounds of ammo—to execute high-impact skills.

| Skill Name | Base Power | Primary Effect | Resource Cost |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Knife Combat** | Moderate | Generic Pierce damage | 0 Ammo |
| **Tommy Burst** | High | Multi-hit Rupture application | 2-4 Ammo |
| **Gut Jab** | 8 Base | Inflicts Wounded status | 0 Ammo |
| **Unload** | Massive | Spends all ammo for burst | All Remaining |
| **Evade** | Defensive | Steals 32 rounds on success | N/A |

The passive ability, **Double Punishment**, is the core of this unit's utility. It creates a feedback loop between Rupture and Wounded status effects. On hit against a target with Rupture but no Wounded, it inflicts one Wounded. Conversely, hitting a target with Wounded but no Rupture inflicts three Rupture. This makes the Southern Thumb Mafia a "Rupture Demon," capable of stacking massive damage multipliers in a single turn.

<Callout type="success" title="Meta Tip">
The "Unload" skill is currently one of the most powerful finishers in the game. By spending all 32 rounds, you inflict Rupture potency equal to the ammo spent divided by two on the following turn, creating an inevitable kill window.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Combat Mechanics: Rupture and Wounded Synergy">

Understanding the interaction between Rupture and Wounded is essential for mastering the **project mirror labyrinth walpurgisnacht** content. While Bleed deals damage based on coin flips, Rupture deals fixed damage every time the target is hit. The new Walpurgisnacht units take this a step further by introducing "Wounded," which enhances the potency of Rupture application.

| Status Effect | Mechanic | Strategic Value |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Rupture** | Fixed damage per hit | Best for multi-hit skills |
| **Wounded** | Multiplies Rupture gain | Essential for boss encounters |
| **Bleed** | Damage on skill use | Secondary support damage |
| **Overheat** | Penalty for ammo usage | Resource management gate |

When building a team for the Walpurgisnacht challenges, you must balance your "Ammo Overheat" count. Using skills like "Unload" provides massive short-term gain but can leave the unit vulnerable if the "Evade" skill fails to replenish the ammo pool. The goal is to reach a state of 20+ Rupture potency, which effectively shreds the HP bars of even the toughest bosses in the Mirror Labyrinth.

<Callout type="warning" title="Resource Management">
Be careful with the Overheat count. While the Southern Thumb Mafia is powerful, failing to manage your ammo surplus can lead to reduced coin power in prolonged battles.
</Callout>
