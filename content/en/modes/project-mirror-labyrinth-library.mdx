Combat in the labyrinth is turn-based and revolves around playing cards from your hand to counter enemy actions. Understanding the balance between offensive and defensive cards is the difference between a successful run and a premature exit.

| Card Category | Primary Function | Strategic Use |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Attack Cards** | Deal direct damage to enemies | Prioritize when enemies are vulnerable or low on health. |
| **Defense Cards** | Mitigate incoming damage/Block | Essential for surviving heavy hits from elite enemies. |
| **Utility Cards** | Buff stats or draw more cards | Use to set up powerful combos in subsequent turns. |
| **EGO Cards** | High-impact special abilities | Save for boss encounters or clearing difficult waves. |

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Reflections, Outfits, and Progression">

The "Gacha" element of the game is handled through **Reflections**. By spending Fragments, you can pull for **Outfits**, which act as your character's "class" or "build," providing unique stats and passive abilities.

### Progression Currencies

| Currency | Primary Source | Main Use |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Fragments** | Codes, Dungeon Runs | Pulling for Reflections, learning new skills. |
| **Condense** | Special Rewards, Milestones | Used in the **Defragmenting** process for Outfits. |
| **Seeds of Light** | High-level Rewards | Unlocking advanced passive abilities and upgrades. |
| **Reflection Tickets** | Events, Codes | Free pulls on Targeted or Standard banners. |
