<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics and Card Synergies">

The heart of **project mirror labyrinth luxcavation** lies in its card combat. Unlike standard RPGs where you might just spam your strongest skill, this game requires careful management of your hand and energy. You must balance offensive pressure with defensive stability. The influence of *Slay the Spire* is evident here: you can often see what the enemy is planning to do, allowing you to react accordingly.

**Card Categories:**
- **Attack Cards**: Used to deplete enemy HP. High-value attacks often come with secondary effects like Bleed or Burn.
- **Defense Cards**: Essential for mitigating incoming damage. Building a high "Block" value can negate entire enemy turns.
- **Utility/EGO Cards**: Special cards that offer powerful buffs or massive AOE damage but usually require specific conditions or higher resource costs.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Analyze Enemy Intent">
    Look at the icons above the enemy. They indicate whether the enemy will attack, defend, or use a debuff on the next turn.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Prioritize Defense">
    If an enemy is preparing a massive hit, ensure you play enough Defense cards to cover your HP. Taking "chip damage" adds up quickly in a roguelite.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Chain Combos">
    Certain cards gain bonuses when played after others. Look for keywords in card descriptions to maximize your damage-per-energy ratio.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Collect EGO Gifts">
    As you progress through the dungeon, you will find EGO Gifts. These are permanent buffs for the current run that can drastically change your strategy.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Deck Thinning Tip">
In deck builders, a smaller, more consistent deck is often better than a large one filled with mediocre cards. Use opportunities to "remove" basic cards to ensure you draw your powerful Outfit-specific cards more often.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Reflections and Outfit Progression">

The rewards earned from **project mirror labyrinth luxcavation** are primarily used in the Reflection system. Reflections are how you obtain new "Outfits," which act as character classes or skins with unique stats and passive abilities. Understanding how to spend your Fragments is key to long-term success.

There are two main types of Reflections:
1. **Standard Reflections**: A general pool of all available Outfits. Good for new players looking to expand their roster.
2. **Targeted Reflections**: These have a higher chance of dropping specific Outfits. Save your Fragments for these when you want to complete a specific build.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Earn Fragments">
    Complete Luxcavation runs or redeem active codes to build up a surplus of Fragments.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Choose a Banner">
    Check the current Reflection banners. If a "Targeted" banner features an Outfit that fits your playstyle, focus your spending there.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Defragment Outfits">
    Once you have an Outfit, use **Condense** and **Seeds of Light** to "Defragment" it. This process unlocks powerful passive abilities and boosts base stats.
  </Step>
</Steps>
