<Section icon={Swords} title="Gameplay Methods to Gain Fragments">

While promotional codes provide a quick influx of currency, you will need consistent in-game methods to sustain your progression over time. Active gameplay loops are designed to reward strategic deck-building and successful exploration.

### 1. Completing Dungeon Runs
Dungeon runs are the core gameplay loop of Project Mirror Labyrinth. To initiate a run, you can either select **Play** from your TAB menu or walk up to and interact with the physical **NO ENTRY** gate in the lobby. 

- **Survival Rewards**: The further you advance through the maze-like stages, the more Fragments you secure at the end of the run.
- **Deck Optimization**: Balancing your deck with a healthy mix of high-damage attack cards and reliable defense cards ensures you survive deeper encounters, maximizing your payout.

### 2. Luxcavation Farming
Luxcavations serve as specialized daily or weekly challenge encounters that offer concentrated rewards. Prioritizing these runs when they are available is highly recommended for players looking to optimize their weekly Fragment income.

### 3. Defragmenting Outfits
As you accumulate duplicate Outfits from Standard and Targeted Reflections, you can utilize the Defragmentation system. While Defragmenting primarily consumes materials like Condense and Seeds of Light to unlock passive abilities, managing your duplicate Outfits efficiently ensures your primary resources are never wasted.

| **Targeted Reflections** | Fragments | None | Focused Outfit targeting |
