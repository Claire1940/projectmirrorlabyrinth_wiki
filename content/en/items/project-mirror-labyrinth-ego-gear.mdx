<Section icon={Target} title="Dungeon Entry and Progression Steps">

Starting your first run can be intimidating given the game's atmospheric and mysterious lobby. Follow these steps to ensure you enter the labyrinth prepared for the card-based challenges ahead.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Preparation">
    Launch the game on Roblox and immediately check the left-side menu for the "Codes" button. Enter all active codes to maximize your starting Fragments.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Reflection Pull">
    Navigate to the Reflections menu. Use your Fragments to pull your first few Outfits. Even low-rarity Outfits are better than the default starter gear for surviving the first few floors.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Launching the Run">
    You have two choices: Press **TAB** and select **Play**, or walk to the physical **NO ENTRY** gate in the lobby. Both will initiate the dungeon matchmaking process.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Deck Construction">
    As you progress through the maze, you will be prompted to choose cards. Balance your deck with at least 40% defensive cards to mitigate the heavy damage dealt by "Urban Plague" level enemies.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Collecting EGO Gifts">
    Look for special nodes in the dungeon labeled as Luxcavation or Gift encounters. These provide the **project mirror labyrinth ego gear** buffs necessary for boss encounters.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Combat Strategy">
Prioritize "Block" and "Evade" cards early in a run. Since health does not fully regenerate between every encounter, avoiding damage is more sustainable than trying to out-damage enemies in a race to the bottom.
</Callout>
