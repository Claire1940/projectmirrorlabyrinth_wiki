<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics: Card Types and Synergies">

Combat in Project Mirror Labyrinth is turn-based and card-driven. Your deck is a living entity that grows or withers based on your choices during a dungeon run. Understanding the distinction between card roles is vital for surviving the later stages of the Urban Plague and beyond.

### Essential Card Categories

| Card Type | Primary Function | Strategic Use |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Attack** | Deal direct damage to enemies. | Use when enemy defense is low or to finish off threats quickly. |
| **Defense** | Mitigate incoming damage/Block. | Mandatory for surviving boss "nuke" turns; always keep 2 in hand. |
| **Utility** | Buffs, card draw, or resource gain. | Essential for cycling your deck to find your "Win Condition" cards. |
| **EGO** | High-cost, high-impact finishers. | Requires EGO Tickets or specific progression to unlock full potential. |

<Callout type="success" title="Deck Building Tip">
