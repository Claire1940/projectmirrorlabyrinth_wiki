<Section icon={Sparkles} title="EGO Upgrade and Thread Spinning Guide">

To unlock the true potential of your EGOs, you must invest in the Thread Spinning system. Upgrading an EGO increases its base clash power, adds additional targets to its attacks, and enhances the passive effects that remain active after the EGO is deployed.

For most S-Tier EGOs, reaching Thread Spin 3 is the minimum requirement for serious content. Upgrading to Thread Spin 4 should be prioritized for your primary damage dealers and main utility supports, as it often increases the target cap for area-of-effect skills.

<Callout type="tip" title="Resource Management Tip">
Do not spread your Thread too thin. Fully upgrading one S-Tier EGO is vastly superior to partially upgrading three B-Tier EGOs.
</Callout>
