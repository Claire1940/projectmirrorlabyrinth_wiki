<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat & Deck Building Strategies">

Dungeon runs in Project Mirror Labyrinth live and die by your deck-building decisions. Because the game utilizes roguelite mechanics, the cards you draft during a run will dictate your survivability and damage output. A common mistake among beginners is drafting only high-damage attack cards, leaving them completely vulnerable to heavy enemy retaliation.

To build a consistent deck, you must understand the primary card archetypes and how they interact under different combat scenarios.

| Archetype | Core Mechanism | Strengths | Weaknesses |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Offensive | High-damage attack cards | Fast dungeon clears, bypasses slow fights | Vulnerable to high-damage counterattacks [1] |
| Defensive | Shielding and block cards | Extremely safe, highly beginner-friendly | Slow progression, susceptible to fatigue [1] |
| Combo | Interconnected card effects | High scaling, explosive turn potential | Requires specific cards, high draft RNG [1] |
