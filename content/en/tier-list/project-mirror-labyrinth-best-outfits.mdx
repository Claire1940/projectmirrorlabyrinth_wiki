<Section icon={Sparkles} title="How to Obtain and Upgrade Your Outfits">

Acquiring the best gear requires a combination of active gameplay and smart resource management. Fragments are the lifeblood of the Reflection system. While you earn these through dungeon runs, using active codes is the fastest way for beginners to jumpstart their collection.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Collect Fragments and Tickets">
    Use active codes like **OfficeDelay** or **UrbanPlague** to instantly gain thousands of Fragments. These are used in the Reflections menu to pull for new outfits.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Access the Reflection Menu">
    From the main lobby, navigate to the Reflections section. Choose between **Targeted Reflections** (if you want a specific featured outfit) or **Standard Reflections**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Analyze Outfit Passives">
    Once an outfit is pulled, check its passive abilities. These often require specific conditions to trigger during combat, such as having a certain amount of "Condense" or playing specific card combos.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Initiate Defragmenting">
    Use **Seeds of Light** and **Condense** materials to upgrade your outfits. This process, known as Defragmenting, is the only way to reach the maximum potential of your gear.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Starter Bonus">
New players should prioritize the code **Welcome**. It provides 350 Fragments and 3 Seeds of Light, which are essential for your first major outfit upgrade.
</Callout>
