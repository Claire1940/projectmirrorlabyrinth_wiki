### The Passive System Explained
Passives are divided into distinct categories based on how they are obtained and where they can be used. To build an effective character, you must understand how to manage your Attribution Points (ATP) and unlock slots.

| Passive Type | How to Unlock | Primary Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Attributable Passives** | Unlocked via Outfits and Defragmentation | Standard equippable passives that cost ATP to slot. |
| **Killing Unlockables** | Defeat specific enemy types in dungeons | Grants targeted slayer bonuses against faction enemies. |
| **Quest Unlockables** | Complete NPC tasks (e.g., Courier runs) | Grants specialized utility and speed passives. |
| **Labyrinth Only** | Found during active dungeon runs | Temporary run-specific buffs that do not persist in the lobby. |

<Callout type="tip" title="Attribution Points (ATP) Warning">
Every standard passive requires a set amount of ATP to equip. Even if you unlock an S-tier passive, you cannot slot it unless your outfit has enough available ATP capacity. Prioritize upgrading your primary outfits to expand your ATP pool.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="How to Unlock and Upgrade Passives">

Unlocking the true potential of your **project mirror labyrinth passives** requires progressing through the game's outfit development system. You cannot simply buy passives; you must earn them by pulling Outfits from Reflections and defragmenting them.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire Outfits from Reflections">
    Use your Fragments to pull Outfits from either Targeted or Standard Reflections. Outfits serve as the physical containers for your permanent passives.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Gather Upgrade Materials">
    Collect essential currencies including Condense and Seeds of Light. These are rarely awarded via active promotional codes or earned through high-tier dungeon clears.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Defragment Your Outfits">
    Navigate to the Outfit menu and select Defragment. Spend your Condense and Seeds of Light to upgrade the outfit, which directly unlocks its locked passive abilities.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Attribute Passives in the Lobby">
    Open your Passives menu in the lobby before entering a dungeon. Allocate your available ATP to equip the unlocked passives onto your active build.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Resource Management">
Condense and Seeds of Light are highly limited resources. Do not spread your materials thin by defragmenting low-tier starter outfits. Save your upgrade materials for SR or meta-relevant outfits that offer high-value passives.
</Callout>

</Section>
