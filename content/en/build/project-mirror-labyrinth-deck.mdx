<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step: Constructing Your First Deck">

Building a viable **project mirror labyrinth deck** for the first time can be overwhelming. Follow these steps to ensure you have a solid foundation before heading into the deeper levels of the labyrinth.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Claim Starter Rewards">
    Launch the game and immediately use the active codes like **UrbanPlague** and **Welcome**. This gives you the Fragments necessary to pull your first set of Reflections.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Equip Your Best Outfit">
    Check your inventory for any Outfits gained from Reflections. Equip the one with the highest rarity or the passives that match your preferred playstyle (Aggressive vs. Defensive).
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Enter the Dungeon">
    Press **TAB** and select "Play" or walk through the **NO ENTRY** gate in the lobby. Your initial deck will be determined by your base stats and Outfit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Draft Cards Strategically">
    After each combat encounter, you will be offered a choice of cards. Always pick cards that complement your Outfit's passives. If you have a defensive Outfit, look for cards that grant "Block."
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Defragment and Upgrade">
    Once you finish a run (win or lose), use your earned Condense and Seeds of Light to Defragment your Outfit. This unlocks permanent passive buffs that make your deck stronger for the next run.
  </Step>
</Steps>
