<Section icon={Swords} title="Core Mechanics and Card Selection">

Building a successful deck requires understanding how different cards interact with your chosen Outfit. In a **project mirror labyrinth poise build**, your primary focus should be on cards that provide "Block" or "Protection" stacks. These stacks are the foundation of your poise, ensuring that even the most aggressive enemies struggle to chip away at your health.

The roguelite nature of the game means you won't always get the perfect cards. However, focusing on the following card categories will stabilize your performance:

1.  **Pure Block Cards**: These are your bread and butter. They provide immediate damage reduction and often scale with your defense stats.
2.  **Defensive Counters**: Cards that provide a small amount of block but also deal damage or apply debuffs when you are hit.
3.  **Resource Generators**: Cards that grant Fragments or Condense-related buffs, allowing you to sustain your deck's momentum.
