<Section icon={Swords} title="Core Deck Construction and Card Combinations">

A successful **Project Mirror Labyrinth Rupture build** requires a finely tuned deck. You want a 2:1 ratio of multi-hit attack cards to high-value defense cards. Because Rupture damage triggers on every hit, a card that hits three times for 5 damage is significantly better than a card that hits once for 15 damage once the enemy has 10+ Rupture stacks.

| Card Name (Type) | Effect | Why It's Used |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Rapid Thrust (Atk)** | 3 Hits, Low Base Dmg | Triggers Rupture 3 times per play |
| **Mirror Shard (Atk)** | Applies 4 Rupture | Primary stack generator for bosses |
| **Glass Shield (Def)** | Block + Counter Hit | Triggers Rupture during enemy turn |
| **Shatter (Skill)** | Consumes 5 Rupture for 2x Dmg | Finishers for low-health enemies |
| **Echoing Strike (Atk)** | Repeats last Atk card | Doubles your Rupture application |

The interaction between "Attack" and "Defense" cards in Project Mirror Labyrinth is unique. If you use a defensive card that has a "Counter" keyword, the hit dealt during the enemy's turn will still trigger Rupture damage. This allows you to deal damage even while playing defensively, which is the hallmark of a pro-level player.
