<Section icon={Swords} title="EGO Gift Categories and Tier Priorities">

Not all EGO Gifts are created equal. In Project Mirror Labyrinth, gifts are generally categorized into three main archetypes: Offensive, Defensive, and Utility. Your priority should shift based on your current Outfit's strengths and the specific dungeon floor you are tackling.

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="Offensive Gifts">
    - **Damage Multipliers**: Increases base power of attack cards.
    - **Status Infliction**: Adds Burn, Bleed, or Tremor to hits.
    - **Crit Chance**: Boosts the likelihood of high-damage rolls.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Defensive Gifts">
    - **Block Retention**: Keeps a portion of your block for the next turn.
    - **HP Regen**: Heals a percentage of health after every combat.
    - **Damage Reduction**: Flat percentage reduction from enemy hits.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Utility Gifts">
    - **Card Draw**: Increases the number of cards drawn per turn.
    - **Resource Gain**: Provides extra Fragments or Condense.
    - **Cost Reduction**: Lowers the energy cost of high-tier cards.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

When building your loadout, aim for a "Core Synergy." If your Outfit relies on defensive counter-attacks, prioritize gifts that enhance Block or provide thorns-like damage. If you are running a glass-cannon build, focus entirely on speed and card draw to end fights before enemies can react.

| Gift Tier | Recommended Focus | Practical Use |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **S-Tier** | Card Draw & Energy | Essential for maintaining combat momentum |
| **A-Tier** | Percentage Damage | Scalable power for late-game bosses |
| **B-Tier** | Flat Defense | Useful for early-game survival |
| **C-Tier** | Resource Buffs | Only pick if you are farming Fragments |
